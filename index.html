Cybersecurity

In a digital-first world, security is everyone's responsibility. Here are the core practices I advocate for staying safe online.

🔐

Password Safety

Use long, complex passphrases and a trusted password manager. Enable 2FA and never reuse passwords across multiple accounts.

🎣

Phishing Awareness

Always verify sender addresses. Never click suspicious links or download unexpected email attachments, even from known contacts.

🛡️

Safe Browsing

Ensure websites use HTTPS. Avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions, and keep your browser and extensions rigorously updated.

👁️

Social Media Privacy

Limit the personal footprint you share publicly. Routinely audit third-party app permissions and platform privacy settings.

📱

OTP & Scam Awareness

Never share One-Time Passwords (OTPs), PINs, or bank details. Beware of urgent, fear-inducing calls or messages demanding immediate action.
